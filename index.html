"What I Stand For IsWhat I Stand On"

Sethu institute of technology along with YEI (Youth EXNORA International) has launched the SIT YOUTH EXNORA ECO CLUB on 8/10/11 . YEI Madurai has joined hands with NSS of SIT, Kariapatti and college’s Quality Circle Team- OASIS to initiate the concept of green campus.The main aim of the club is make SIT GREEN by various projects like solid waste management, campus cleaning and developing the youth.Later it was renamed as SIT ECO CLUB and it is functioning in that name by doing various activities.
